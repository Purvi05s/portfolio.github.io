About Me

Hi, I’m Purvi Chhaya, a dedicated Website Designer based in London, United Kingdom, with over 8 years of experience crafting dynamic, user-centric websites. My passion lies in creating seamless and visually appealing designs for corporate websites and e-commerce platforms. Whether it’s enhancing user experience or driving conversions, I design with a strategic approach that aligns with my clients' business goals.

Over the years, I’ve worked with a diverse range of businesses, helping them establish a strong online presence. My expertise includes building responsive websites using a combination of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React and Bootstrap. I also leverage WordPress for creating flexible, easy-to-manage sites, and use Figma for delivering pixel-perfect designs.

I thrive on collaborating closely with clients to bring their vision to life, ensuring every project is both visually appealing and functional. I stay up to date with the latest design trends and technologies, ensuring that each website I create not only meets but exceeds industry standards.
